Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I need more agents than my plan allows?

You can upgrade at any time. Additional agents on Starter/Growth are $1,500/mo each. Or move to Enterprise for unlimited.

Can I choose which AI models my agents use?

Growth and Enterprise plans let you select from Claude, GPT-4, Gemini, and open-source models. Starter uses our optimized default stack.

Is there a setup fee?

No. The shaping call is free, and we handle all onboarding. Your agents can be live within 48 hours.

What if the agents make mistakes?

All agent actions are logged and reviewable on the Paperclip dashboard. You can set approval gates for sensitive operations. Agents learn from corrections over time.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Month-to-month billing, cancel anytime with 30-day notice. Annual plans get 2 months free.
