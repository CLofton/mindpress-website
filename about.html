About MindPress

Operator-led AI consulting for the work behind the business.

MindPress exists to help companies move beyond AI experiments and install reliable AI workflows into finance, people operations, tax, EOS, sales, and custom operating systems.

Origin

Built from operating complexity, not AI novelty.

MindPress was founded by Chris Lofton after years of operating large education programs across hundreds of locations and instructors. At that scale, every back-office delay compounds: payroll, reporting, hiring, forecasting, compliance, and management cadence all become leverage points.

The insight behind MindPress is direct: the most valuable AI work is often inside the repeatable processes that already run the company. Those workflows need consulting discipline, system integration, controls, and adoption support.
