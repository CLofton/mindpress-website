Our Mission

The traditional company is broken. Departments of 20 people doing work that intelligent systems could handle faster, cheaper, and around the clock. We're not replacing humans — we're freeing them to do what only humans can: create, lead, and innovate.

MindPress deploys autonomous AI agent teams that handle the operational backbone of your company — engineering, marketing, operations, product management — while your human team focuses on strategy and relationships.

Built on the open-source Paperclip control plane, MindPress brings enterprise-grade AI workforce management to companies of every size. From 3-person startups to 10,000-employee enterprises, we make AI agents actually work — not just demo well.
