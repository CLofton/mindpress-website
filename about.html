The Beginning

From 240+ Locations to AI Agent Teams

MindPress was founded by Chris Lofton, who spent years managing education programs across 240+ locations with over 550+ instructors. At that scale, every operational inefficiency compounds — payroll errors cascade, HR bottlenecks multiply, and financial reporting becomes a full-time job.

Chris saw firsthand that the work of entire departments — accounting, HR, tax, FP&A — followed repeatable patterns. Patterns that humans executed reliably but slowly, and at enormous cost. The question wasn't whether AI could do this work. It was: why isn't it already?

MindPress is the answer. We take the operational playbooks that run real businesses and deploy AI agent teams that execute them — faster, cheaper, and around the clock.

Mission

Replace Departments with AI Agent Teams

Every company runs on the same core departments — Accounting, HR, Tax, FP&A. These departments follow documented processes, handle structured data, and produce predictable outputs. That's exactly what AI agents excel at. Our mission is to make world-class back-office operations accessible to every business through AI — eliminating the cost, complexity, and headcount of traditional departments.
