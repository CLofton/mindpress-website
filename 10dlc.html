FAQ

Common Questions

Q

What is 10DLC and why do I need it?

10DLC (10-Digit Long Code) is the mandatory registration system for businesses sending text messages in the US. As of February 2025, all carriers block unregistered business SMS. If you text customers from a business number, you need 10DLC registration.

Q

How fast will my registration be approved?

Our AI gets your submission ready in under an hour. TCR and carrier approval typically takes 2-5 business days with a pre-validated submission, vs. 2-6 weeks with manual registration and rejections.

Q

What if my registration gets rejected?

Our pre-validation catches 95%+ of rejection causes before submission. If a rejection does occur, our AI parses the error, generates a fix, and resubmits — often within minutes.

Q

Do I need to be technical to use this?

Not at all. If you can paste a URL and type your EIN, you can use 10DLC Express. We've eliminated all telecom jargon and technical complexity.

Q

Which carriers are supported?

We submit to all three major US carriers: T-Mobile, AT&T, and Verizon — simultaneously, with independent tracking for each.
