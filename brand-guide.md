# MindPress Brand Guide

## Brand Positioning

MindPress is an AI services and consulting firm for companies that want operational AI deployed into real finance, people, tax, sales, and management workflows. The brand should feel executive, precise, and implementation-ready: less "AI hype," more "trusted operating partner."

MindPress is a sister brand to Austin AI School. It keeps the shared signal of deep navy, cyan, mint, and amber, but shifts the system toward a more corporate consulting palette with cobalt, teal, brass, and graphite.

## Typography

Primary typeface: `Inter`.

Fallback stack: `Inter, ui-sans-serif, system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", sans-serif`.

Usage:
- Headlines: 800 weight, tight line height, no negative letter spacing.
- Body: 400 to 500 weight, generous line height.
- Navigation and labels: 620 to 760 weight, concise phrasing.
- Buttons: 700 weight, action-oriented copy.

## Logo Concept

The MindPress mark is a geometric neural aperture. Four colored nodes connect through a diamond frame into a central white core, representing expert models, business systems, human judgment, and measurable outputs being pressed into one operating system.

The square frame gives the mark a consulting-grade structure. The asymmetric node colors connect it to Austin AI School without copying that brand directly.

## Brand Voice

MindPress speaks like a senior operating partner:
- Precise, measured, and board-ready.
- Outcome-first: margin, close speed, compliance, pipeline, accountability.
- Confident without hype.
- Specific about where AI fits and where controls, review, and escalation remain essential.
- Direct CTAs: "Book a Consultation," "Start AI Intake," "Map the First Workflow."

Avoid:
- Generic AI futurism.
- Replacement language that sounds reckless or adversarial.
- Overclaiming autonomy in regulated workflows.
- Casual startup slang.

## Messaging Pillars

1. Practical AI deployment: We install AI into existing systems and workflows.
2. Controls by design: Every workflow includes review paths, audit trails, and escalation logic.
3. Operator-led consulting: Recommendations come from how businesses actually run.
4. Measurable lift: Every engagement is tied to cycle time, error reduction, margin, or pipeline impact.

## Service Architecture

Core service lines:
- AI Accounting
- AI HR
- AI Tax
- AI FP&A
- EOS Integration
- Sales and Lead Generation
- Custom AI Services

## Visual Style

Use dark, quiet backgrounds with high-contrast text, fine borders, geometric diagrams, and restrained accent color. Avoid decorative blobs, novelty icons, and overly playful AI imagery. The system should feel premium, rigorous, and ready for executive review.
